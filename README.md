# CSGO-Skinomics: Digital Assets vs. Traditional Markets

## Project Overview
This project performs an **econometric analysis** of the digital asset market within the Counter-Strike (CS:GO/CS2) ecosystem. Using data analytics, I explore price volatility, inflation rates, and market trends of in-game items, comparing them against traditional financial benchmarks like the **S&P 500** and the **Consumer Price Index (CPI)**.

## Research Objectives
* **Asset Correlation:** Analyzing if high-tier digital skins act as a hedge against real-world inflation.
* **Market Volatility:** Measuring price fluctuations triggered by game updates and major tournament (Major) cycles.
* **Arbitrage Opportunities:** Identifying price discrepancies between the Steam Community Market and third-party platforms (Eneba, Skinport, etc.).

## Tech Stack
* **SQL (BigQuery/Kaggle):** For data extraction and complex window functions (moving averages, rankings).
* **Python:** For data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and visualization.
* **Statistics:** Applying regression models to predict price trends based on supply scarcity and demand shocks.
